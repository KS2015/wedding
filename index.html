KYLE & TARA

Kyle and Tara met through mutual friends in 2011. Kyle was going into his sophomore year and Tara was starting her freshman year of high school. After meeting, both knew the connection was strong. They spent their high school years watching each other play sports, going to dances, hanging out with friends, and enjoying each others company.


After high school, they both attended Owens Community College. Tara graduated with an Associate Degree in Diagnostic Medical Sonography and Kyle graduated with an Associate Degree in Computer Programming. After graduating, they moved out on their own to an apartment to start their lives. Around a year later, they purchased their first home together in Perrysburg, OH. Kyle and Tara have similar interests in life and are a great team together. Their sense of humor is one that leaves everyone smiling (except Tara think's she's funnier).


THE PROPOSAL

January 14, 2019
New Orleans, Louisiana (N.O.L.A)
(Yes, we named our dog after this)

Kyle and Tara had just landed in New Orleans, LA the morning after spending the weekend in Clearwater, Florida visting family. Traveling to New Orleans was a first for both Kyle and Tara and both were excited to explore the city. The first night there, they wanted to experience some of night life. After dining at one of local restaurants (which we thought was some of the best food of their lives), they headed back to the hotel to enjoy some drinks appetizers at the popular lobby bar.



The hotel had a rooftop pool, bar and an amazing view of the city so both of them were excited to get up there and view the city skyline for the first time. Upon heading up to the bar, we noticed that no one was on the roof due to the colder weather, which was actually warmer temps for them! While viewing the breathtaking skyline, Tara turned around completely shocked and thrown off guard and saw Kyle there on one knee. The rest of the night, and the vacation, was spent celebrating each other and their future together.
